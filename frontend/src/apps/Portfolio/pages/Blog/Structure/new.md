## What Is Golpo AI?

Golpo AI is a whiteboard explainer video generator built by two Stanford CS brothers — Shraman and Shreyas Kar — and backed by Y Combinator (YC S25 batch). It was founded in 2024 and has already attracted investment from Afore Capital and Emergence Equity.

The core idea is simple: you paste a prompt or upload a document (PDF, GitHub repo, Jira board, Google Doc), and Golpo turns it into a fully narrated, whiteboard-style animated video — automatically. No editing skills, no After Effects, no recording setup.

It directly solves a problem that tools like Sora and Veo 3 don't: **practical communication**. Sora makes cinematic clips. Golpo explains things. Huge difference.

**Who it's built for:**
- YouTubers creating tutorial or explainer content
- Educators turning lesson notes into videos
- Developers documenting APIs and workflows
- Coaches and course creators
- Corporate trainers and onboarding teams

## How Golpo AI Actually Works (Step by Step)

1. Go to [video.golpoai.com](https://video.golpoai.com?via=dev-code-space)
2. Sign up free — you get 1 credit (= 1 minute of video)
3. Type your topic or paste a document into the prompt box
4. Choose your style: **Golpo Sketch** (black & white line art) or **Golpo Canvas** (older 2.0 style)
5. Set your voice tone, accent, and language (50+ supported)
6. Hit generate — video is ready in minutes
7. Watch in browser... and then hit the paywall to download 😅

The generation itself is genuinely fast — under 5 minutes for a 1-minute video. The sketch-style animation looks clean and professional, not like a cheap slideshow.

## What's Actually Good About Golpo AI

### 1. The Output Quality Is Real
The whiteboard sketch animation looks genuinely professional. Unlike cheap text-to-video tools that slap stock footage together, Golpo draws custom animations that match your content. For developer tutorials and tech explainers, the visual style works extremely well.

### 2. Document-to-Video Is Genuinely Useful
This is Golpo's killer feature. You can paste a GitHub README, upload a PDF, or even link a Jira board — and it produces a coherent explainer video. No other tool does this as well right now.

### 3. 50+ Languages Supported
For non-English creators — Hindi, Spanish, Bengali, French — this is huge. You can create content in your native language with AI voiceover.

### 4. Voice Customization
You can describe how the voice should sound: "British accent", "talk like a professor", "energetic and fast". On paid plans, you can clone your own voice entirely.

### 5. Fast Generation
Under 5 minutes for most videos. For a 75-second explainer, that's genuinely impressive.

---

## What's Actually Bad About Golpo AI

### 1. Can't Download on Free Plan
This is the biggest frustration. You generate a great video and then hit a wall. There's not even a "share to YouTube" free option — just a "Copy URL" that links back to their platform.

### 2. Expensive for the Output Volume
$39/month for 20 minutes of video is $2 per minute. If you're a high-volume creator posting weekly, you'll burn through credits fast and hit unexpected add-on costs.

### 3. No Offline Access / Limited Integrations
As a newer tool, native integrations with Notion, Slack, or LMS platforms are limited. You download, re-upload manually.

### 4. Pricing Isn't Region-Sensitive
$39 in the US is very different from ₹3,200 in India. Even the founders acknowledged this in their Product Hunt comments. No PPP (purchasing power parity) pricing yet.

### 5. Free Plan Is Essentially a Demo
1 credit with no download isn't really a free plan — it's a trial to see the output quality before paying.
